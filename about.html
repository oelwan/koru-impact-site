About Koru Impact

To serve as a catalyst for sustainable human and organizational flourishing by integrating evidence-based research, systemic empathy, and purpose-driven strategies that empower individuals, teams, and communities to thrive in a rapidly changing world.

Who We Are

Koru Impact is a strategic consulting company dedicated to advancing well-being, learning, and social impact. Our work is grounded in three core values: Evidence-Based Rigor, Systemic Empathy, and Purpose-Driven Impact. We believe in the power of research, compassion, and purposeful action to create lasting change.

Our Four Core Pillars

  • Social Science Research: Rigorous, evidence-based research to inform strategies for organizations and communities.
  • B2B Well-being & Psychological Services: Tailored services to foster resilience, engagement, and holistic growth for teams and leaders.
  • Professional Education & Content Distribution: Transformative learning experiences and high-impact content for professionals and organizations.
  • UN SDG & Social Impact Consulting: Aligning strategies with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals for maximum positive impact.

Founder: Şener Cem Irmak

Şener Cem Irmak is a social scientist, educator, and consultant with a passion for fostering human flourishing and organizational transformation. With a background in psychology, education, and systems thinking, Cem has led research initiatives, designed well-being programs, and advised organizations on aligning with the UN Sustainable Development Goals. His approach blends scientific rigor with deep empathy and a commitment to meaningful, purpose-driven impact. Cem’s work is recognized for its integrative perspective, bridging research, practice, and policy to create sustainable change.
